FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your account. Your access continues until the end of the billing period. Note that we do not offer refunds — see our Refund Policy.
How do skins work?
Once subscribed to Premium or Lifetime Premium, go to the Settings page to equip any of the 3 exclusive skins. Your choice is saved and active in every run.
Do extra lives stack with continues?
Premium subscribers start each run with 5 lives and get up to 4 ad-based continues, compared to 3 lives and 2 continues on the free plan.
Is there a refund if I change my mind?
All purchases are final and non-refundable as stated in our Refund Policy. Please review all terms before subscribing.
What payment methods are accepted?
Payments are processed securely via Paddle — accepting all major credit cards, PayPal, and other regional payment methods. Payment integration coming soon.
Does Lifetime include future content?
Lifetime Basic includes all future ad-removal features. Lifetime Premium includes all future premium skins and features — a true one-time payment for everything.
