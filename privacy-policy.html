PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: February 2026 · Effective immediately · Applies to all Alien Escape services

Summary: Alien Escape is a browser-based game. We collect minimal data — primarily for game functionality (local high scores) and, if you subscribe, for billing purposes via Paddle. We do not sell your data. Third-party ad partners (Adsterra) may use cookies to serve relevant ads to free-tier users.

1. WHO WE ARE

Alien Escape ("we," "us," "our") is the operator of the Alien Escape web game available at this domain. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at the address listed in Section 9.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Automatically via your browser: Standard web server logs may capture your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and date/time of visit. This data is used for security and diagnostic purposes only.
  • Locally in your browser: We use localStorage to store your game high score, selected skin, audio preferences, and subscription status. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • When you subscribe (via Paddle): If you purchase a subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Paddle.com. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not store your full payment card details. Paddle may collect your name, email address, and billing information under their own privacy policy.
  • Cookies from third-party advertisers: Free-tier users may receive interest-based advertising from Adsterra. These ads use cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve relevant advertisements. See Section 4 for details.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To operate and improve the game experience
  • To verify subscription status and grant premium features
  • To display relevant advertisements to free-tier users (not applicable to paid subscribers)
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. THIRD-PARTY ADVERTISING (ADSTERRA)

We use Adsterra to display advertisements on the free tier of Alien Escape. Adsterra and its advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Measure ad performance and frequency
  • Serve interest-based (personalized) advertisements
  • Analyze traffic and engagement

You can opt out of interest-based advertising via Adsterra's opt-out page, or by using standard browser privacy tools. Paid subscribers have all advertisements removed and are not subject to ad tracking through our platform.

Adsterra's privacy policy: https://www.adsterra.com/privacy-policy/

5. PADDLE — PAYMENT PROCESSING

All subscription payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited (Paddle), which acts as our Merchant of Record. This means Paddle processes payments, handles VAT/tax compliance, and is the entity appearing on your billing statement. Paddle's privacy policy governs all data collected during checkout. We recommend reviewing it at paddle.com/legal/privacy.

6. DATA RETENTION

Game data stored in your browser's localStorage is retained until you clear your browser data or explicitly reset it in settings. If you have a Paddle subscription, transaction records are retained as required by Paddle and applicable financial regulations. Server logs are purged after 90 days.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a portable format

For most game data, you can exercise these rights directly by clearing your browser localStorage. For subscription-related data, please contact us or Paddle directly.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Alien Escape is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete that information.
